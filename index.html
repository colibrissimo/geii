Upcycling: E-Waste to Object

Second-year electrical-engineering students take a box of dead electronics and turn it into something that works, and has a soul. 2025 to 2026.

Each project began as salvage: recovered circuit boards, components desoldered by hand, wire and scrap. The brief was simple. Reuse what would have been thrown away, build an object, and show how it was made.
